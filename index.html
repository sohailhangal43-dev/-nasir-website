About Us

Nasir Hangal Motor Rewinding Works is a trusted motor rewinding and repair shop in Margao, Goa. We repair and rewind all types of electric motors and water pumps with proper care and quality work.

We handle single-phase and three-phase motors, submersible pumps, monoblock pumps, fan motors, and industrial motors. If your motor is not working, overheating, or making noise, we check and fix the problem correctly.

We use good quality copper wire and test every motor before delivery. Our focus is on honest service, timely work, and reasonable pricing.
