About Us

Nasir Hangal Motor Rewinding Works is a trusted motor rewinding and repair shop located in Margao, Goa. We specialize in repairing and rewinding all types of electric motors and water pumps with proper care and high-quality workmanship.

We handle single-phase and three-phase motors, water pumps, submersible pumps, monoblock pumps, fan motors, and industrial motors. If your motor is not working, overheating, or making unusual noise, we carefully inspect and fix the issue correctly.

We use high-quality copper wire and test every motor before delivery to ensure performance and durability. Our focus is on honest service, timely completion, and reasonable pricing.
